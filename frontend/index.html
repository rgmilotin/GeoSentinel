MINING / SALT MINING SECTOR

Salt mines and active mines.
Complete underground monitoring.

Romania has active salt mines in Praid, Ocna Dej, Cacica and Slanic, coal mines in the Jiu Valley and uranium operations at Crucea-Botusana — all with critical monitoring needs that remain digitally uncovered.

📡
Near-real-time Sentinel-1 InSAR
We automatically process Copernicus EGMS data for any location in Romania. We detect subsidence from ±1mm/month with a 12-day revisit. Validated: relative error <5% compared with classical leveling.
🌊
Multi-layer sensor network
MEMS geophones (0.1–80Hz, 24-bit, ~$200–800/node), IoT sensors for gases and infiltration, GNSS RTK for absolute geodesy. LoRaWAN/4G communication, <2W/node consumption.
🧠
AI agent for signal fusion
Isolation Forest + LSTM Autoencoder on fused time series (seismic + InSAR + hydro + weather). RAG over ITM/ANRM incident databases. Alerts with automatically generated context.
📋
Geodetic risk report
We automatically generate briefs for ITM inspectors: risk level, causal hypothesis and concrete recommendations. Auditable timestamped PDF format. Official deliverable for the authorized person.
🗺️
Geodetic sensor-placement analysis
We use EGMS + Copernicus DEM + ANRM geology to determine optimal physical sensor positions. Output: GIS map with prioritized risk polygons.
🔄
Backtesting on real cases
Validated on retrospective EGMS 2019–2023 data for the Praid area. The system would have generated alerts days before documented events. Generalization demonstrated on international cases.

TUNNELS / INFRASTRUCTURE SECTOR

Highway tunnels.
Intelligent structural monitoring.

Romania is now building the largest tunnel pipeline in its history: Meses (A3), Margina-Holdea (A1), Sibiu-Pitesti and the Unirii Motorway (A8). Each one requires geotechnical monitoring throughout construction and operation.

📐
Lining convergence and deformation
Inclinometers, extensometers, strain gauges and wireless convergometers. We detect tunnel convergence with sub-millimeter precision. Automatic alerts if the rate exceeds thresholds from European standards (EUROCODE 7).
🔬
Periodic terrestrial LiDAR
Full 3D tunnel scans at defined intervals. We compare point clouds and automatically detect deformation zones, cracks or geometric changes. ±1–5mm precision, full cross-section coverage.
💧
Infiltration and water monitoring
Piezometers for water pressure in the rock mass, wall humidity sensors and active leak detectors. We correlate with ANM precipitation and INHGA levels for post-rain infiltration prediction.
🌍
InSAR for surface subsidence
We monitor the ground surface above the tunnel using Sentinel-1 data. We detect excavation-induced subsidence, settlement of adjacent structures and deformation along the motorway corridor. Free through EGMS.
⚡
Vibrations and dynamic loads
Accelerometers for monitoring vibrations generated by traffic, blasting or earthquakes. We compare against regulatory limits. Real-time warning when safety thresholds are exceeded.
📊
SHM report for operator and designer
Complete Structural Health Monitoring: current status, trends, comparison with serviceability and ultimate limits. CNAIR/CNIR-ready format, exportable for an authorized geotechnical expert.
